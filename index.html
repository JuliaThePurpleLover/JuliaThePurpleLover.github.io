Math Poem

Math is fun, Math is cool, Math helps you with its tools.
It’s all around you and there for you to use,
there are so many ways to use it too.
Here are some ways to use it’s tools you can add apples,
divide to share your cookies,
multiply your money, or even subtract sandwiches.
Using math makes you smarter although it can be a
bother never give up and always try try again and maybe even harder.
There are signs to help show you what to do
and lots of numbers to count through.
Math follows you all through school, it also follows you through life too.
So keep calm and carry on cause math is fun,
math is cool and math helps you with its tools.
